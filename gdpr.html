GDPR Policy

Our company, LeadsPartner, is committed to protecting the personal data of our customers and employees in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy outlines our approach to complying with the GDPR and ensuring the protection of personal data.

Data Collection, Processing, and Retention

We collect personal data only for specific, explicit, and legitimate purposes, and we will not process or retain this data longer than necessary to achieve these purposes. We will obtain clear and informed consent from individuals before collecting or processing their personal data.

Data Access and Rectification

Individuals have the right to access the personal data that we hold about them and to request that we rectify or delete any inaccurate data. We will respond to these requests within the time frame required by the GDPR.

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure, or access. We also conduct regular reviews and audits to ensure that our data security measures remain effective.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify the appropriate authorities and affected individuals without undue delay, in accordance with the GDPR.

Data Protection Officer (DPO)

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) who is responsible for ensuring our compliance with the GDPR and for providing guidance and advice on data protection matters. The contact information of our DPO is dpo@leadspartner.sg
